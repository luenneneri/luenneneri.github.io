    • XLIX

    a narrative for a last game day

    For this book project, we would have to design and publish a 160-page pocket-sized book that described a narrative of our daily commute.
    Living in a New School dorm, close to all of the New School buildings, can turn a daily routine into something uninteresting. So, as a NFL fan, I have decided to portray something I have done since I got to New York City: watching a New England Patriots game on Sunday.
    Luckily, that was the weekend of the XLIX Superbowl, so my image-based book would register the narrative of the last game day, with some friends, at a sports bar at the Village.
