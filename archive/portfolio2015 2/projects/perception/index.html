    is the medium

    layout of a booklet
    about Bridget Riley

    This project regards the layout solution for a big excerpt from “The Eye’s Mind: Bridget Riley Collected Writings 1965–1999” (London: Thames and Hudson, 1999) entitled “Perception Is the Medium”, that was produced during a Digital Layouts course.
    The design was intended to be clean and to promote an easy reading. Both images and text were given and the cover, the size, the layout and the binding were up to the designers.
