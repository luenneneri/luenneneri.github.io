    about

    Hey, there! My name is Luenne, I'm 23 and was born and raised in São Paulo, Brazil.
    I’m an architecture student at the University of São Paulo who just got tired and moved to New York to study Communication Design at The New School under the grace of a one-year scholarship provided by the Brazilian government.
    Fun facts: I enjoy myself being a designer better than being an architect, I consider myself someone politically engaged, I’m into design works that have social impacts and, shocker, my favorite color is yellow.
    Born in São Paulo and passionate about New York, I’d say there’s nothing more soothing for me as the noise of the traffic coming from the windows.
