    mockup study for Artist’s Book

    This two-minute book was the first mockup made in class for an example of an Artist’s Book. It was supposed to document an exercise that we had to propose to ourselves.
    Therefore, I chose to create a color palette for each of the book piles in my visual field that stood at the tables of the NYU Bobst Library and identify them according to their main subjects.
    Through the two-minute book structure I was able to divide the book in two, categorizing it in bright and sober colors.
