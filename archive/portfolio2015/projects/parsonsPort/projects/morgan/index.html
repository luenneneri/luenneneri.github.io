    design of book about a street artist

    To tell the story of this musician, this book was made so that he could relate it to his life and his passion, so we organized some fun stories he told us, identified them in some songs and designed it in the size of a cd.
    As we talked we could perceive that he was very passionate about what he does. Morgan plays in bars and venues, but he claims the places he likes playing the most are on the street. A lot of people pass by. They are not expecting him, they only stop because they really want to hear him.
